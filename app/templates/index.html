TestFORGE is a web-application that lets you create, take and evaluate multiple choice tests from various categories.

Select a topic, choose from multiple subtopics and enter the desired ammount of questions. Or just enter the ammount of questions an test your knowlegde on a random selection of all categories.

More Features

Not only results

Getting results is good. Getting helpful comments is better. If available, they show up when you chose a wrong answer or missed a right one.

Statistics

As a registered user you have access to the profile page, where you can read some statistics, track your progress and see how you perform against other users.

Save your tests.

If you enjoyed a test or feel you could do better, you can simply save it to your user profile and repeat it whenever you want.

Add new content

Apply as a maintainer and add new topics, subtopics, questions and answers.

Edit or delete

Make changes to questions and answers, add additional options or delete a whole topic or subtopic.

Insights

See what you have in stock and what users care about. You might want to add some new questions where the demand is high.

Manage users

As an admin you can easily create new users, promote them for higher roles like maintainer or admin and - if neccessary - delete them.
